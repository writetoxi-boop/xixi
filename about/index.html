Our Story

XIXI was born from a conviction: that Asian culture, creativity, and capital are not a niche story — they are the story of our era. And yet, for too long, the platforms built to celebrate culture have looked the other way.

We exist to change that. XIXI is a Gen Z luxury media platform spotlighting the forces shaping Asia and, by extension, the world. Fashion that bends the rules. Art that refuses translation. Founders that move faster than the press can write. Wealth that doesn't announce itself.

"The world is looking East. We've been here the whole time."

Our editorial philosophy is simple: report with precision, write with heat, platform with purpose. We celebrate what is, not what the West assumes Asia to be. We don't romanticise. We don't exoticise. We document a present that is extraordinary on its own terms.

XIXI publishes across five verticals — Fashion, Art, Culture, Capital, and Power — and produces The Asia Letter, a weekly newsletter read by 12,000+ globally curious readers who want to understand what's moving before it becomes mainstream.
